Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to make it easier for people that are interested in rafting to find and plan an adventure. Also make it easier to find new customers.

Audience

The intended audience is those that want to go on a rafting adventure. Either on their mobile phones or desktop to plan a trip with destination and raft rental. This will be available for people of the age between 12 and 65 that have the financial means of renting a raft and planning a trip either solo or in a group.
