Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to make it easier for people that are interested in rafting to find and plan an adventure. Also make it easier to find new customers.

Audience

The intended audience is those of the age between 12 and 65 that have the financial means of renting a raft and planning a trip either solo or in a group.
